# The Past Two Years (2024-2025)

The past two years have been extraordinarily productive and transformative.
A significant cognitive shift in early 2024 opened up new capacities
for mathematical understanding and cross-domain thinking that I hadn't
previously experienced. This led to:

- 800+ pages of mathematics notes covering real analysis, abstract
  algebra, measure-theoretic probability, and convex optimization
- 53 AI special lectures at universities and corporations
- Founding Erudio Bio Korea, Inc.
- Building K-PAI into a recognized Silicon Valley-Korea AI community
- Gates Foundation grant for Erudio Bio's cancer diagnostics platform
- SNUBH research partnership (something a mentor said was impossible)
- KFAS-Salzburg Global Leadership Initiative Fellowship
- Significant media exposure and growing reputation
- High-profile speaking engagements including Seoul Shilla Hotel
  (K-Science Academy) and MIT GSW 2026

The philosophical foundation underlying this period: resolving the
existential question not by finding intrinsic meaning, but by
consciously choosing to create meaning as a fully autonomous agent.
This is documented in "[{{ agent_living_the_question.title }}]({{ agent_living_the_question.url }}){:target="_blank"}"
(January 2025).

# What I've Noticed About Myself

The same intensity that drove extraordinary productivity has also
created patterns worth attending to:

- Sustained very high energy and activity for two years
- Difficulty with quiet time and single-tasking
- Need for constant stimulation while working
- Friends have expressed concern about burnout for about 6 months
- Recent loss of motivation and interest across multiple domains
  including Erudio Bio, K-PAI, and the planned book project

# Philosophical Foundation

Through deep exploration of both Western philosophy (Socrates, Plato,
Aristotle, Kant, Hume, Mill, Husserl, Sartre, Camus, Rawls) and
Eastern traditions (Buddhism, Taoism, Confucianism) combined with
rigorous mathematical thinking, I've reached a stable philosophical
position:

- There is no intrinsic predetermined meaning to life
- This is not nihilism - it is the foundation for [genuine autonomy]({{ agent_living_the_question.url }}){:target="_blank"}
- Meaning is something I consciously choose to create
- This choice is itself the most authentic expression of freedom
- The past two years are evidence that this chosen meaning can
  generate real impact

This foundation is stable. External recognition neither inflates
nor deflates it.

# Core Motivation Going Forward

What genuinely resonates:

**Erudio Bio, Inc.** - Providing inexpensive cancer diagnostics that
allow earlier detection, reducing human suffering and public health
costs. Gates Foundation validation confirms the mission has genuine
systemic impact potential. We are at a unique historical moment where
AI maturity, biotech readiness, and demographic need are converging.
My cross-domain understanding positions me to see and act on this
convergence in ways that are genuinely rare.

**The "Great Convergence" thesis** - Population aging + AI maturity +
biotech readiness = a trillion-dollar opportunity that also genuinely
improves human lives. This is where my integrated expertise has
maximum leverage.

# The Core Insight

The past two years demonstrated what becomes possible when integrated
wisdom meets conscious purpose. The next phase requires something
different: not intensity, but depth. Not breadth of activity, but
clarity of focus.

The mission is clear: help build technology that reduces human
suffering from cancer, at a moment in history when this is finally
possible.

That is enough. That is more than enough.
